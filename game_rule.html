Island Game

Control the Character

Use WASD to control the characters to move up, left, down, and right.

Move close to a tree/stone/brush. Long press the left button of the mouse to control the character to cut it.

Items will drop when tree/stone/brush is broken.Get close to drops to collect them

About inventory

The top column is the player's inventory.

Press 'I' to expand the inventory.

In the inventory, long press the current item to see the description of this item.

You can drag the items in the inventory to a blank grid

The mouse wheel can switch the items held by the character.

please try to ensure that the items in the warehouse are arranged continuously, and there is no empty grid between the item and the previous item, otherwise it will affect the choice when crafting

About crafting

There are two ways of crafting.

Normal Crafting

•Press the 'C' key to enter the normal synthesis scene

•You can choose 2-3 items for synthesis (except for level 4 items) and get corresponding points according to the level of the items obtained after synthesis.

•The level of the product is determined by the average level of the composites in your synthetic items. The collection can be used for synthesis, but does not affect the final product level, only the product category.

•Press 'R' key to select the items in the first grid, 'T' to select the second grid, and 'Y' to select the third.

•Press 'E' key to generate things

Risky Crafting

•presses the V key to enter the luck synthesis scene

•You can only choose two items to synthesize, and the first one must be the synthesis product A, and the second must be the collectionB

•There is a 50% chance that item C will be generated directly. The level of C is 1 level higher than the level of A, and the score is twice the score obtained for generating C level

•There is a 50% chance of getting nothing

•Press 'R' key to select the items in the first grid, and 'T' to select the second grid.

•Press 'E' key to generate things

Before game start

Lv.1 items is 4 points, Lv.2 is 8 points, Lv.3 is 16 points, and Lv.4 is 32 points

Level 4 items have no categories and cannot be used for crafting

The collection has no categories, but the collection has a bias that affects the category of the synthetic product

The category of product is determined by the collection and the proportion of items in the composition formula

After entering the game screen, you can scale the browser screen to the appropriate size

Just try to get as higher mark as possible !
